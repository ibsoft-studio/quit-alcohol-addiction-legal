Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my PRO purchase after reinstalling?
Open the app and go to the Paywall / Upgrade screen. Tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID or Google account you used when you originally purchased. Your PRO access will be restored immediately at no charge.
I purchased PRO but the app still shows free content. What do I do?
First, try tapping Restore Purchases on the paywall screen. If that doesn't work, close and reopen the app fully. If the issue persists, make sure your device has an internet connection, then try restarting. Still not working? Email us at ibsoft.studio@gmail.com with your Apple/Google account email and we'll sort it out.
How do I cancel my subscription?
iOS: Open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → find Soleo: Quit Alcohol → tap Cancel Subscription.

Android: Open the Google Play app → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → find Soleo → tap Cancel subscription.

You'll keep PRO access until the end of your current billing period.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled directly by Apple or Google, not by us.

iOS: Go to reportaproblem.apple.com, find your purchase, and request a refund.

Android: Open the Google Play app → tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Budget & order history → find the order → Request a refund.
What's included in the free version vs. PRO?
Free: Chapter 1 (7 articles, flashcards & quiz), the live sobriety timer, and limited craving log entries.

PRO: All 20 chapters (140 articles), unlimited craving tracking, full analytics & heatmaps, all 7-day recovery challenges, all 5 breathing exercises, all themes, and all future content updates.
I accidentally logged a drink (slip). Can I undo it?
Yes. Go to the Timer / Streak tab. You'll find an option to edit or reset your sobriety start date. Logging a slip is intentional — the app is designed to help you keep going without shame, not to judge. You can always adjust and continue your journey.
Does the app work offline?
Yes. All content — articles, flashcards, breathing exercises, and the sobriety timer — works fully offline. Your data is stored locally on your device. An internet connection is only needed when purchasing or restoring PRO access.
Will I lose my data if I delete and reinstall the app?
Currently, your progress (sobriety start date, craving logs, XP) is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will delete this data. We recommend noting your sobriety start date before uninstalling so you can re-enter it after reinstalling. Your PRO purchase is always restorable via Restore Purchases.
The app crashed or isn't loading. What should I try?
1. Close the app fully and reopen it.
2. Restart your device.
3. Check if an app update is available in the App Store or Google Play.
4. If the problem persists, uninstall and reinstall the app (note your sobriety date first).

If nothing works, please email us at ibsoft.studio@gmail.com with your device model and iOS/Android version so we can investigate.
Is this app a replacement for medical treatment?
No. Soleo is a self-help education and habit-tracking tool. It is not a substitute for professional medical care. If you are experiencing severe withdrawal symptoms — such as seizures, hallucinations, or severe shaking — please seek emergency medical care immediately. Alcohol withdrawal can be life-threatening.
